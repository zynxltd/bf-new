# Navigate to AutoSSL (Not CSR Generation)

## You're Currently On:
- **"Generated Certificate Signing Request"** page
- This is for **manual** certificate installation
- **Not what you need** for Let's Encrypt

## What You Need:
- **AutoSSL** - Automatic Let's Encrypt installation

## Steps to Get to AutoSSL:

### Option 1: Use cPanel Search
1. **Click in the search bar** at the top right of cPanel
2. **Type:** `AutoSSL`
3. **Click on:** "SSL/TLS Status" or "AutoSSL"

### Option 2: Navigate Manually
1. **In the left sidebar**, look for **"Security"** section
2. **Click on:** "Security" to expand it
3. **Click on:** "SSL/TLS Status" or "AutoSSL"

### Option 3: Direct Navigation
1. **Look for "Security"** in the main cPanel dashboard
2. **Find:** "SSL/TLS Status" or "AutoSSL" icon
3. **Click it**

## Once You're on AutoSSL Page:

1. **You should see:**
   - A list of your domains
   - Status of SSL certificates
   - A **"Run AutoSSL"** button

2. **Click "Run AutoSSL"** button

3. **Wait 1-2 minutes** - it will automatically:
   - Generate Let's Encrypt certificate
   - Install it
   - Set up auto-renewal

## What AutoSSL Page Looks Like:
- Title: "SSL/TLS Status" or "AutoSSL"
- Shows domains with SSL status
- Has a **"Run AutoSSL"** or **"Run AutoSSL for All Domains"** button
- **NOT** a CSR generation form

## If You Can't Find AutoSSL:
- Contact your hosting provider
- Ask them to enable AutoSSL for your account
- Or ask them to install Let's Encrypt certificate for `bloomingfast.com`
